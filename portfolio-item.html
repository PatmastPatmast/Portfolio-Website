Crystal Frost

The Hunters

Every tribe boasts individuals endowed with inherent strength, and the Hunters of Crystal Frost are no exception. Manifesting exceptional physical prowess from a young age, these individuals showcase remarkable talent that becomes a beacon of protection in the perilous, frigid world our citizens inhabit. Amidst the chilling challenges of their environment, the Hunters naturally evolve into society's guardians.

In order to fulfill their role as protectors, these individuals undergo rigorous training, mastering the art of wielding the heaviest prosthetics. These prosthetics, with their formidable weight and inherent power, present a formidable challenge to their bearers. The journey to mastery is not only a test of physical strength but also a testament to the resilience and fortitude required to harness their latent capabilities. Additionally, the high cost of these prosthetics further underscores the dedication and sacrifice made by the Hunters.

Embracing their responsibility to safeguard their fellow citizens, the Hunters emerge as celebrated heroes within the community. Stepping outside the confines of safety, they venture into the treacherous wilderness to confront and vanquish the looming threats posed by formidable beasts. Through their unwavering commitment and daring exploits, the Hunters symbolize a collective shield, ensuring the safety and well-being of all who call Crystal Frost home.
