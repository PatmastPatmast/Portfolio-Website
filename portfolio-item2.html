Crystal Frost

The Runners

In a world dominated by ice and cold, the necessity for swift transportation across vast distances becomes apparent. The ingenious engineers of Crystal Frost, renowned for their intellect, opt for a unique solution: rather than grappling with the challenges of domesticating creatures, they construct machines reminiscent of animals. Taking inspiration from larger feline forms, these mechanical marvels, known as "runners," offer an efficient means of traversing expansive landscapes.

Resembling majestic big cats, these runners are equipped with coal-made noses that serve as markers, aiding in precise coordination during their journeys. While the mechanism itself may not boast extraordinary complexity, its undeniable efficiency and practicality render it indispensable in the daily lives of the Crystal Frost populace.

The runners, though unremarkable in appearance, prove to be a testament to the resourcefulness of the Crystal Frost engineers. Through their innovative approach, they have created a solution that not only addresses the need for rapid transportation in the icy terrain but also exemplifies the seamless integration of technology into the fabric of their society.
