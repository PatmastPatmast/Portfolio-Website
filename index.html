Who I am

Artist – Designer

I’m a concept artist and visual storyteller working across character design, fashion, and cultural research. My work bridges visual development with cultural narratives, exploring how clothing, mythology, and identity intersect.

With a background in East Asian Studies and a strong foundation in digital painting and design, I work on projects that range from editorial worldbuilding to narrative concept art. I'm especially interested in fashion as a medium of storytelling and emotional symbolism.

I’m currently developing a visual platform exploring speculative fashion and cultural memory.
